Faq in NGS

  • 1. Which Next Generation services does Credence Genomics offer?

    FUNGAL AND BACTERIAL SEQUENCING

    • 16s rRNA gene sequencing of clinical, agricultural, food and beverage, soil, pharmaceutical, cosmetic, veterinary marine and seawater and other ecological samples

    • Sequencing of ITS region of above said samples

    RESEARCH

    • Testing for all types of cancers

    • We also facilitate Next Generation Sequencing projects and training programmes for all universities and academic institutions.

  • 2. How can I order your next generation sequencing service?

    Please “contact us” (with a link redirecting the user to the contact us page on our site) with your specific project requirements and details.

  • 3. Which is the sequencing method you use?

    At Credence Genomics we routinely perform our Next Generation Sequencing services on Ion Torrent semi-conductor sequencing platform. You can learn more about our sequencing platform by visiting “our technology”

  • 4. How fast can I get the results?

    Results for BactFast® and FungiFast® tests are usually generated within 48 hours. However, lenght of time taken to process your samples depend on the number of samples you provide and the type of assay you order. We will discuss the project timeline with you once we recieve the final project confirmation.

  • 5. What is are the DNA requirements for sequencing?

    We require 20ng of total DNA preferably stored in TE buffer for testing. Unused DNA can be returned to investigators, following completion of the project. In case of clinical samples, we accept whole blood, tumor samples and Formalin Fixed Paraffin Embedded (FFPE) tissues.

  • 6. Can you detect any genetically inherited disease using Next Generation Sequencing?

    Yes, we can. We make use of customized test panels exclusively desinged to test combinations of genes of your interest. All you have to do is to send us the list of genes, specifying your requirements and we will get it sequenced for you.
